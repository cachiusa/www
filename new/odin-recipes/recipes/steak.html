Steak Hash

Description

This tasty steak hash was simple to make, tasted fantastic, and we both loved it! I know what I’ll be making with our leftover steak from now on!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of potatoes, diced
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 1½ – 2 cups of leftover steak, diced
  • 2 tbsp leftover caramelized onion, optional
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • Sea salt and freshly cracked pepper, to taste
  • Paprika, to taste
  • 3 steamed eggs, if desired
  • 1 green onion, chopped for garnish

Steps

  1. Sauté the steak, seasoned to taste, in olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat for about 3-4 minutes per side, or to your desired degree of doneness.
  2. Remove from heat and let it rest for 5-7 minutes before dicing.
  3. Boil the diced potatoes in water for 4-5 minutes; drain.
  4. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat
  5. Add the onion and sauté for 2 minutes, then add the potatoes
  6. Push the potatoes down in the pan with your spatula and leave them alone for 3-4 minutes. Flip the potatoes to brown on the other side. Continue to leave the potatoes alone for 3-4 minutes before flipping.
  7. Once the potatoes and onions are golden brown, add the diced steak and caramelized onions and heat through.
  8. Season with paprika, sea salt, and freshly cracked pepper, to taste.
  9. Serve your steak hash with an egg on top. Serve immediately. Enjoy.
